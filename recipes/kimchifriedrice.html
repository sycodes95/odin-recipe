Kevin's Kimchi Fried Rice

An easy to make meal that tastes great and is nutritionally well rounded. Often tastes better when hammered.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of chopped kimchi (required)
  • 2 Cups of cooked white rice (required)
  • 1 Whole chopped onion (required)
  • A couple of chopped green onions (however much you want) (required)
  • 1/2 Cup of ground beef (can substitute ground beef with meat of your choice) (required)
  • 1 Egg (required)
  • Pinch of sesame seeds (optional)
  • Sesame oil (optional)
  • Canola oil (required)
  • Salt (required)
  • Pepper (required)
  • Love (required)

Directions

  1. Drizzle some canola oil onto heated pan on high heat
  2. Pour your chopped kimchi and chopped onions onto the pan
  3. Stir fry for 30 seconds - 1 minute
  4. Add the rice and meat onto the pan
  5. Throw a couple pinches of salt in there
  6. Pepper it up as much as you like
  7. Cook an egg sunny side up on a different pan
  8. Stir fry until meat is cooked well done and rice has desired texture
  9. When egg is finished, place on top of fried rice
  10. Drizzle some sesame oil
  11. Pinch of sesame seeds
